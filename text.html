Mango Pancakes

Preparation : 10m | Cook : 20m | Serves : 4

Ingredients (9)

  • 500g pkt frozen diced mango, defrosted
  • 200g (1 1/3 cups) self-raising flour
  • 2 tbsp caster sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg,lightly whisked
  • 250 ml (1 cup) milk
  • 20g butter, melted
  • Finely grated lime rind (optional)
  • Coconut flavored yoghurt, to serve

Method

Step-1
Place half the mango in a food processor and process until smooth.

Step-2
Place the flour, sugar and baking powder in a bowl. Use a whisk to combine. Make a well in the centre. Add the egg and milk. Whisk until almost combined. Whisk in the mango puree and butter until smooth.

Step-3
Heat a large non-stick frying pan over medium-low heat. Spray with oil. Pour 80ml (1/3 cup) of the batter into the pan. Cook for 1-2 minutes or until bubbles appear on the surface. Turn and cook for a further minute or until golden. Transfer to a baking tray. Cover loosely with foil and place in the oven to keep warm. Repeat with the remaining batter to make 12 pancakes.

Step-4
Serve the pancakes with the remaining mango, sprinkled with lime, if using, and topped with yoghurt.

Allergens : May contain Gluten, Wheat, Egg, Milk, Lactose

Cream of Cauliflower Soup

Preparation : 25m | Cook : 35m | Serves : 6

Ingredients (8)

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large brown onion, finely chopped
  • 3 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 large head (1.5kg) white cauliflower, trimmed, cut into small florets
  • 4 cups chicken stock or vegetable stock
  • 1/2 cup pouring cream
  • Chopped Chives, to serve
  • Extra pouring cream, to serve

Method

Step-1
Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion. Reduce heat to medium-low. Cook, uncovered, stirring occasionally, for 10 minutes or until onion is soft. Stir in cumin seeds. Cook, stirring, for 1 minute.

Step-2
Add cauliflower and stock to pan. Cover and bring to the boil. Reduce heat to low. Cook, partially covered, for 20 minutes or until cauliflower is very tender. Remove from heat. Allow to cool for 10 minutes.

Step-3
Process soup, in batches, until smooth. Return soup to pan over low heat. Stir in cream. Cook, stirring, for 4 to 5 minutes or until heated through. Season with salt.

Step-4
Ladle soup into bowls. Drizzle with extra cream. Top with chives and season with cracked black pepper. Serve.

Allergens : May contain Yeast, Milk, Lactose

Creamy Mushroom Gnocchi

Preparation : 5m | Cook : 10m | Serves : 4

Ingredients (5)

  • 400g mixed mushrooms (such as cup, brown or shiitake), thinly sliced
  • 120g kale & baby spinach leaves
  • 300ml thickened cream
  • 1 tbsp wholegrain mustard
  • 500g pkt potato gnocchi

Method

Step-1
Spray a non-stick frying pan with olive oil spray. Heat over high heat. Add the mushroom and cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 mins or until the mushroom begins to brown. Add the kale and spinach, cream and mustard and cook, stirring, for 2-3 mins or until the spinach just wilts.

Step-2
Meanwhile, cook the gnocchi in a saucepan of boiling water following packet directions. Drain well.

Step-3
Add the gnocchi to the mushroom mixture in the pan and carefully toss to combine. Season.

Step-4
Divide the gnocchi mixture evenly among serving bowls.

Allergens : May contain Gluten, Wheat, Mustard, Milk, Lactose

Ratatouille Rice Bake

Preparation : 5m | Cook : 15m | Serves : 4

Ingredients (6)

  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 750g pkt Coles Kitchen Ratatouille
  • 510g jar Coles Simply Tomato, Onion & Garlic Pasta Sauce
  • 3 x 250g pkt Coles Microwave White Long Grain Rice
  • 70g (1/2 cup) store-bought shredded parmesan
  • Fresh basil leaves, to serve (optional)

Method

Step-1
Heat oil in a large, non-stick ovenproof frying pan over medium-high heat. Add ratatouille and cook, stirring, for 5 minutes or until almost cooked through. Add pasta sauce and stir to combine. Season, then bring to a simmer.

Step-2
Squeeze the packets of microwave rice to separate the grains. Add rice to the ratatouille mixture and stir to combine, breaking up any large lumps of rice with a wooden spoon. Cook, stirring, for 2-3 minutes or until the rice is heated through. Smooth the surface and turn off the heat.

Step-3
Preheat an oven grill to high. Sprinkle parmesan over the rice mixture. Grill for 2-3 minutes or until the cheese is golden and bubbling. Sprinkle with basil leaves, if using, to serve. Watch our step-by-step video below to see how to make our 5-ingredient ratatouille rice bake.

Allergens : May contain Milk, Lactose

Ambriosa Eton Mess

Preparation : 10m | Serves : 8

Ingredients (10)

  • 250ml (1 cup) thickened cream
  • 300g sour cream
  • 2 tbsp icing sugar mixture
  • 2 x 100g pkt pink and white mini marshmallows
  • 500g fresh strawberries, hulled, coarsely chopped
  • 450g can pineapple pieces in juice, drained
  • 65g (1 cup) McKenzie's Shredded Coconut
  • 310g can Coles Mandarin Segments in Syrup, drained
  • 80g (8-pack) meringue nests, coarsely crushed
  • Maraschino cherries, to serve (optional)

Method

Step-1
Use electric beaters to beat thickened cream, sour cream and icing sugar in a bowl until soft peaks form. Transfer to a large bowl (see tip, above).

Step-2
Add marshmallows, strawberry, pineapple and coconut to the bowl and mix until well combined. Gently fold through mandarin. Spoon half the fruit mixture into eight 330ml serving glasses. Top with half the meringue. Repeat layering with the remaining fruit mixture and meringue. Top each glass with a cherry (if using), to serve.
